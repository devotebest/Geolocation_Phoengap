PLEASE UPDATE YOUR APP BEFORE VISITING THE ARTS TRAIL

If there are any last-minute changes to the trail, this app will be updated to include details. Please update this app just before you visit the trail to make sure that you have the most up-to-date information.

Android

  • Find the Google Play Store icon from the app drawer or your home screen and launch it.
  • In the Play Store, tap on the 'Menu' icon. You will find the 'My Apps' option listed. Tap on it to see all of the apps you have installed.
  • Look down the list for SBA Trail and tap the update button next to it.
